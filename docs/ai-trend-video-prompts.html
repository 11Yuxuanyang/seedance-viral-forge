Trend Traffic

How to turn trends into AI short-video prompts

Trend videos only work when the topic is still warm, the visual is immediately legible, and the discussion hook survives outside the original meme context.

Home Trend skill

What the skill optimizes for

This repository treats trend prompting as a traffic job. The skill scores candidate directions by heat, visual potential, discussion value, and fit with proven video patterns before it writes the final prompt.

What it avoids

The repository explicitly filters out trends that are already cold, not visual enough, politically sensitive, or dependent on directly imitating real people in unsafe ways.

Why reusable patterns matter

Most traffic ideas fail because they rely on one fragile joke. This repo keeps a library mindset: parallel universe, character crossover, impossible confrontation, and viral structure remake. The pattern survives even when the topic rotates.
