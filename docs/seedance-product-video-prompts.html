Product Selling

How to write Seedance 2.0 product video prompts in Chinese

The main problem with many product prompts is not creativity. It is missing physical detail, weak reveal timing, and no usable workflow for incomplete inputs.

Home Product skill

What this repo does differently

The product-selling skill does not jump straight to a final script. It first extracts the product, category, selling points, and missing physical usage details. Then it proposes three directions, waits for a choice, and only then outputs the final Chinese script.

What inputs matter most

The minimum useful inputs are the product name, category, and one to three key selling points. The strongest extra input is a plain-language explanation of what the product looks like and how it is physically used.

Why this structure works for Seedance

Seedance is more reliable when prompts restate physical details, describe motion as mechanism instead of abstraction, and keep scene changes limited. That is why this repository treats prompt writing as an operational protocol, not as a one-paragraph creative exercise.
